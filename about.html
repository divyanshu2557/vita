SINCE 1994

It started in a small shed with a big vision.

Before Vita was an industry, it was a farmer's promise. Our founder, Sardar Singh, realized that the quality of milk in our villages was dropping because the feed was becoming industrial and chemical-heavy. He decided to go back to the basics—real grain, real minerals, and real honesty.

[Image of a timeline showing the evolution of cattle feed production from manual mixing to modern automation]

Our Journey

1994: The First Batch

We started by mixing small batches of mustard cake and maize for local dairy farmers.

2005: The Lab

Vita established its first quality-testing lab to ensure every bag is toxin-free.

2024: 50,000+ Farmers

Today, we serve the entire northern belt, keeping the health of the animal at the heart of our tech.

"A cow is not a machine; she is a member of the farmer's family. We feed her with that respect."

— Sardar Singh, Founder
